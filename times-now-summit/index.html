Times Now Summit 2024

Amidst astronomical growth and an unwavering trajectory of development across various sectors, India has firmly established itself as a force to be reckoned with. Despite facing diverse challenges including geopolitical uncertainties, economic hurdles, population dynamics, and internal complexities, India has emerged as a beacon of resilience and leadership. As we convene at the Times Now Summit, our focus is to unravel the essence of India's ascent as a global powerhouse, exploring the boundless potential encapsulated in the theme: INDIA UNSTOPPABLE.

About Times Now Summit

At this landmark forum, strategists, policymakers, and influencers accountable for India’s future will converge to celebrate the achievements thus far, take stock and unearth the direction that the nation will take on Government, Politics, Economy, National Security, Health, Diplomacy, Infrastructure, Technology Innovation, and other such aspects that would define our future.

Earlier editions of Times Now Summit were graced by the Hon’ble PM Shri Narendra Modi; Union Ministers Amit Shah, Nirmala Sitharaman, Ravi Shankar Prasad, Piyush Goyal, Nitin Gadkari, Smriti Irani, as well as influential minds like Peter Zeihan, K Sivan and Sri Sri Ravi Shankar amongst others.

For anyone in India who needs to envision, shape and plan for the future, it is vital to anticipate the trajectory of the nation. To seek this clarity in vision of where we are and where we are headed, across every aspect that is important for shaping the nation, Times Network welcomes you to the Times Now Summit.
