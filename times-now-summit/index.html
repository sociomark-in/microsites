Times Now Summit 2024

In this edition, furthering the agenda to shape India’s most comprehensive action plan for progress, Times Now Summit puts the spotlight on India: A Vibrant Democracy, Global Bright Spot. At a time when the world is working around a wavering dollar, uncalculated war, constant geopolitical tensions, volatile crude prices and myriad local challenges, India continues to march forward with a historically enviable global respect, exemplary neighbor relations and a buoyant economy. Each State comes together to strengthen the national agenda, thus making the vibrant democracy of India, a global bright spot.

Government, Policy, Strategic, Geopolitical, Economic stalwarts from home and abroad will converge in

New Delhi on 27th & 28th March at the Times Now Summit.

About Times Now Summit

At this landmark forum, strategists, policymakers, and influencers accountable for India’s future will converge to celebrate the achievements thus far, stock and unearth the direction that the nation will take on Government, Politics, Economy, National Security, Health, Diplomacy, Infrastructure, Technology Innovation, and other such aspects that would define our future.

Earlier editions of Times Now Summit were graced by the Hon’ble PM Shri Narendra Modi; Union Ministers Amit Shah, Nirmala Sitharaman, Ravi Shankar Prasad, Piyush Goyal, Nitin Gadkari, Smriti Irani, as well as influential minds like Peter Zeihan, K Sivan and Sri Sri Ravi Shankar amongst others.

For anyone in India who needs to envision, shape and plan for the future, it is vital to anticipate the trajectory of the nation. To seek this clarity in vision of where we are and where we are headed, across every aspect that is important for shaping the nation, Times Network welcomes you to the Times Now Summit.
